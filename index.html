About JAMFO

Jamuiyat Ar-Rashad Muslim Foundation (JAMFO) is a Muslim foundation that has existed in the past and continues to serve its community through shared responsibility and collective support.

Jamuiyat Ar-Rashad Muslim Foundation functions in a cooperative-friendly manner, where members come together to support one another socially, economically, and morally through collective contributions and shared responsibility.

The Foundation provides:

  • Welfare assistance
  • Educational support
  • Empowerment and self-reliance programs
  • Community development services

These services are offered to Muslims and non-Muslims alike, in the spirit of peace, unity, and social responsibility.

Vision

To build a self-reliant, peaceful, and supportive community through cooperation, moral values, and shared prosperity.

Mission

  • To encourage cooperation and mutual assistance among members
  • To support economic empowerment through collective initiatives
  • To promote education, moral values, and social responsibility
  • To provide welfare support to members and the wider community
  • To foster unity and peaceful co-existence among people of all faiths

Objectives (cooperative-friendly)

The objectives of Jamuiyat Ar-Rashad Muslim Foundation include:

  • Promoting cooperative principles of unity and mutual support
  • Assisting members financially and socially when needed
  • Encouraging savings, contributions, and collective development
  • Supporting education, skills acquisition, and self-reliance
  • Serving society regardless of religious background where necessary
